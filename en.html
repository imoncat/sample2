ABOUT Mio YOGA
"Connecting ancient Yoga wisdom to the present day to enrich people's lives"

Mio MUKAIDA
Since 2003, I have been studying yoga at various ashrams across India, including the Sivananda Ashram,
and have been active as a yoga instructor in both India and Japan.
From 2010 to 2013, I studied abroad in South India, where I specialized in Sanskrit, meditation, and sacred scriptures such as the Bhagavad Gita. Currently,
I have an extensive track record as a yoga therapist,
offering a wide range of expertise in meditation, breathwork, and yoga philosophy.
